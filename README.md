# AI Chemist

**The AGI Philosopher's Stone: AI as Modern Alchemy**

The pursuit of Artificial General Intelligence mirrors the alchemical search for the philosopher's stone — both promise universal transformation while consuming real resources (power, water, minerals, capital) against promissory, always-receding outputs. This paper argues for six structural isomorphisms between AGI and alchemy, and proposes AGS (Artificial General Society) as a reframing analogous to chemistry's emergence from alchemy: reproducible, scoped, and grounded rather than universal-transformation-seeking.
